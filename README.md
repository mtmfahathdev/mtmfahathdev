### Glad to see you here!  
I’m a full-stack developer with a demonstrated history of working in the Software Development industry. Being a full-stack allows me to not only develop client-facing apps and websites but also develop it with cutting edge backend support.

I specialize in building robust backends that do all the heavy lifting for your app or website. I love designing systems that are light yet powerful, distributed yet synchronized and beautiful yet effective. I also frequently blog about the world behind the screen which involves system designs, databases, security, servers, optimisation and also promising technologies like Blockchain and PWAs.
